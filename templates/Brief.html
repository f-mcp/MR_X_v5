Greetings Agents


I'll get straight to the point, we desperately need your help.

The elusive Mr X is causing trouble once again, and I trust I don't need to remind you how dangerous he can be. We need to lock him up once and for all. Find him. Complete this mission by telling me his name.

The good news is we've picked up on his virtual paper trail. Unfortunately, it's highly cryptic and we need the best-of-the-best in code crackers to track him down, hence why we've come to you.

It is believed that he is operating with his three henchman. Mr X is too much of a sneaky bastard to catch him alone, so our plan is to pin down these three and use the informaton we get from them to find Mr X once and for all.

The clues they've left behind are shown within this document. As you know, each requires an input to display vital information for following clues.

When you close in on the henchmen, we have discriptions of their appearance to confirm their identities. These can be found below this brief. Finally, we also found a confusing cipher key left behind by the henchman, see if comes in handy. Find it below the henchmen descriptions.

Best of luck agents, the country's security rests in your hands.

Happy hunting,


M
